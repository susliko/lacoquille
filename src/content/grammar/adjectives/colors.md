## What colors do

Color adjectives describe nouns. Most follow the standard agreement rules, but some stay invariable.

## Regular color adjectives

Most colors agree like normal adjectives:

| Masculine | Feminine | English |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| rouge | rouge | red |
| bleu | bleue | blue |
| vert | verte | green |
| noir | noire | black |
| blanc | blanche | white |
| jaune | jaune | yellow |
| violet | violette | purple |

- *un pull **rouge*** → *une robe **roug****e***
- *des pulls **rouges*** → *des robes **rouges***

## Invariable colors

These colors never change form.

### Noun-turned colors

Some color names come from nouns and stay invariable:

| Color | Origin | Example |
|-------|--------|---------|
| orange | the fruit | *des pulls **orange*** |
| marron | the chestnut | *des murs **marron*** |
| citron | the lemon | *des robes **citron*** |
| chocolat | chocolate | *des murs **chocolat*** |

> But: **rose** (rose = flower adjective) agrees: *une robe **rose***

### Compound colors

Colors made from two words stay invariable:

| Invariable | English |
|------------|---------|
| bleu marine | navy blue |
| bleu ciel | sky blue |
| rouge bordeaux | burgundy |
| vert olive | olive green |
| rouge gorge | robin redbreast |
| brun | brown |

- *des robes **bleu marine*** — navy blue dresses
- *une jupe **bleu ciel*** — a sky blue skirt

## Origin and material colors

Colors derived from materials or origins **do agree**:

| Color | Agrees | Example |
|-------|--------|---------|
| rose | ✓ | *une robe rose* |
| pourpre | ✓ | *une cape pourpre* |
| écarlate | ✓ | *une cape écarlate* |
| incarna | ✓ | *des lèvres incarnat* |

## Quick reference

| Type | Agreement | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Regular (rouge, bleu, vert...) | Agrees | *une robe rouge* |
| Noun colors (marron, orange) | Invariable | *des pulls orange* |
| Compound (bleu marine) | Invariable | *des robes bleu marine* |
| Origin/material (rose, pourpre) | Agrees | *une robe rose* |

## Common mistakes

- ✗ *une voiture verte* is correct ✓ — remember to agree
- ✗ *des pulls marron* is correct ✓ — marron is invariable
- ✗ *des robes bleu marine* is correct ✓ — compound colors stay invariable
