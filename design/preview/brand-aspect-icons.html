completed
A solid dot — the action is done, closed, fully contained.
Tenses: passé composé, futur antérieur, conditionnel passé
ongoing
A wave — the action keeps going, no clear end in view.
Tenses: imparfait (in progress), présent
habitual
A repeated wave — the action happens again and again.
Tenses: imparfait (habitual), présent (routine)
anterior
Dot + arrow — one action happened before another.
Tenses: plus-que-parfait, futur antérieur
